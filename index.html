FACTS

Explosive Growth

By 2030, it's estimated that there will be over 50 billion connected devices worldwide, showcasing the rapid expansion of IoT.

Connected Devices Outnumber Humans

The number of connected devices already exceeds the global human population, illustrating the pervasive nature of IoT in our daily lives.

Industrial IoT (IIoT) Impact

Industrial sectors are leveraging IoT for efficiency; it's predicted that IIoT could contribute $14.2 trillion to the global economy by 2030.
